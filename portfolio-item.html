SolidWorks Car Jack

Scissor Car Jack Assembly

As a side project, I challeneged myself with designing and making a car jack assembly in SolidWorks to improve my 3D modelling skills. This involved designing all 6 parts of the car jack seperately and then combining them into an assembly, which can move like how it would in real life. The fasteners were added after for aesthetic purposes and to simulate how the parts would be assembled in real life.

These are just some of the individual parts that were made which were then used in the assembly. They all have realistic dimensions and can be manufactured or 3D printed to create a real life car jack. The assembly was a success and I learned a lot about how to efficiently model and assemble parts in SolidWorks.
